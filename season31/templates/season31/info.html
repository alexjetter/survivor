What's New This Season?
Contains Minor Spoilers, mouse-over to read
  • Instead of hiding Immunity Idols at camp, this season Immunity Idol Clues will be hidden at camp. Idols will be hidden within the challenges, and Castaways with Clues need to be gutsy enough to snag the Idol during a challenge. (Link)
  • Immunity Idols will vary greatly in appearance, making it potentially hard to convince someone yours is real, and opening the door for fakes. (Link)
  • In addition to the 2 starting tribes (Bayon and Ta Keo), a third tribe (Angkor)has been revealed. At some point they'll re-shuffle people into 3 tribes. (Link)
